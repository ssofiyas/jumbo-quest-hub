# Jumbo Loyalty App Concept – School Project 2026


## View App - https://jumbo-quest-hub.lovable.app

## 1. Project Overview
This project is part of a curriculum challenge focused on designing a digital-first loyalty ecosystem for **Shopping Centre Jumbo**, the market leader in Vantaa, Finland. 

**Our Mission:** To design and validate a high-fidelity app prototype that increases customer retention, rewards frequent visits, and provides actionable consumer data for mall management and tenants.

---

## 2. Business Context & Objectives
Jumbo is currently the #1 mall in Finland. However, to maintain its position against rising e-commerce and local competitors (like Tripla), a personalized digital value proposition is essential.

### Key Goals:
* **Defend Market Leadership:** Strengthen the bond with local customers through a dedicated digital platform.
* **Data-Driven Insights:** Transition from anonymous footfall to identifiable, loyal members.
* **Increase Spend:** Drive higher average transaction values and visit frequency.

---

## 3. Target Audience: "The Curious Regulars" (Segment B)
Through strategic analysis, our team identified **Segment B (17.4% of total visitors)** as our primary target for the loyalty app.

* **Who they are:** Tech-savvy young families and adults (18–39) who are highly active in digital ecosystems.
* **Why them?** While they aren't the largest group by volume (19.0% are Routine-Oriented), they have the highest motivation to use digital coupons, participate in gamification, and make impulse purchases.
* **The Problem Worth Solving:** "Information Overload." They want to find value among 170+ stores but need a centralized, effortless way to track deals and events.

---

## 4. Prototype Functionality (Core Features)

The prototype is built on a clean, professional UI consistent with Jumbo’s brand identity, featuring five main navigation pillars:

###  Home Page (Discovery Hub)
A personalized "shopping command center" featuring a search bar for 170+ stores, a sliding banner with daily highlights, and personalized "Recommended for You" coupons.

### News & Events
A scrollable feed of the latest mall updates, spring fashion previews, and interactive "Instagrammable" events. Users can save news or add events to their personal calendars.

### Digital Wallet (Scan & Cards)
A centralized hub where users can display their **Jumbo QR Code** to earn points. It also acts as a digital manager for partner store loyalty cards (Prisma, Citymarket, etc.) and allows for manual receipt scanning.

### Game Center (Gamification)
An engagement-focused module designed to drive footfall to different zones of the mall.
* **Concept:** "Daily Adventure" stamp collection.
* **Mechanism:** Users earn bonus points and rewards (like free coffee or exclusive discounts) by completing simple interactive tasks within the mall.

### Profile & Rewards (User Dashboard)
A comprehensive, single-page scrollable view for account management:
* **Jumbo Score:** Real-time point tracking and membership tier status (Silver/Gold).
* **Rewards Progress:** A clear table showing how close the user is to their next unlocked benefit.
* **Activity History:** A transparent log of points earned through shopping and visits.
* **Personal & App Settings:** Direct control over contact info, language (EN/FI/SV), and notifications.

---

## 5. Technical Implementation
This prototype was developed by our group using modern design tools (Lovable, Marvel App, Figma) to demonstrate the seamless user journey from discovery to reward. The design prioritizes **scannability**, **minimalism**, and **user-centricity**.
